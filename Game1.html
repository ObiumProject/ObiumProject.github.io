PLOT

The story takes place in an unknown abandoned building filled with traps and bloodthirsty homicidal maniacs; its main characters are a thirteen-year—old girl Rachel Gardner and a twenty-year-old boy named Zach. Each floor of the building has its own owner, the so-called "Angel". The entire floor space is called the play area, within which the "game" is held. The goal of the "Angel" in this game is to catch and kill his victim, the goal of the victim is to escape from the killer and get out of there alive.

COMMUNITY OPINION

Summing up, I can safely say that Angel of Bloodshed, despite some problems with the plot and too easy riddles, is a pretty good game that will definitely attract fans of quests and J-RPG. It has some interesting finds, the characters are interesting to watch, and the final twist is able to surprise and even impress the player. I can't say that immediately after passing, I ran to read the manga of the same name.
